Portfolio Color & Style Guidelines

- 1. Base Colors (Background & Text)

    Background: soft neutral tones

    White: #FFFFFF → clean, timeless

    Light gray: #F5F5F5 → easy on the eyes

    Text: dark but not pure black

    Charcoal: #333333 → softer than black

    Dark gray: #4B4B4B

- 2. Accent Colors (Links, Buttons, Highlights)

    Use 1–2 accent colors maximum

    Subtle, calm tones:

    Soft blue: #1D4ED8 → good for links/icons

    Teal: #0D9488 → fresh but professional

    Warm accent: #F59E0B → small highlights/buttons

    Main accent color for links/buttons, optional secondary for hover effects

- 3. Optional Secondary Palette

    Light neutrals for sections/cards: #E5E7EB

    Slightly darker gray for borders: #D1D5DB

- 4. Typography

    Stick with 1–2 fonts max:

    Sans-serif for body: Inter, Roboto, Helvetica

    Optional serif for headings: Merriweather, Playfair Display

    Keep sizes consistent and minimal style variations

- 5. General Tips

    Lots of white space—avoid cramming elements

    Consistent padding/margins

    Simple hover effects: slightly darker or lighter shade of your accent color
